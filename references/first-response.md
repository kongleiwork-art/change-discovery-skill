## Reduce Scope

> **Classification:** Major if built as requested; Medium if reduced. Confidence: medium-high.
>
> A full community system would touch accounts, content, moderation, notifications, and likely storage. That is a core product expansion with lasting maintenance cost.
>
> **Preliminary product assessment:** Reduce scope.
>
> The stated goal is “make the product less lonely,” but there is little evidence yet that a full community is the right first step. Cost of doing nothing is mainly slower engagement growth, not a broken core workflow. Stronger first step: a lightweight shared-link or feedback channel for existing users, not forums, follows, and moderation.
>
> **Recommended model tier for the reduced scope:** Medium.
>
> The product recommendation above is preliminary; GitHub research and deeper validation have not started yet.
>
> Reply **continue** to keep the current selection and explore the reduced direction, or switch first and then reply **continue**. If you want the full community system anyway, say so explicitly.
